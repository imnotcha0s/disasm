I reverse engineer software for a living and write about the parts that
generalise. Most of what appears here started as a comment in a disassembler
that got too long to leave in the database.

## Contact

Mail is the reliable channel. Everything else is decorative, much like the title
bars on this site.

## Colophon

Built with Hugo and the `disasm` theme — a pastiche of IDA Pro running on
Windows XP. No trademarked icon art is used; every icon is drawn from
rectangles and paths in an SVG sprite. The chrome is a costume over plain
semantic HTML: turn JavaScript off and the site loses a typewriter animation, a
loading splash, and a search dialog, and keeps everything else.
